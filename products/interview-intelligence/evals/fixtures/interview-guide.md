# Interview Guide — Ana Duarte (Senior Platform Engineer) · 60 min (fixture, fictional)

Competencies owned: **Infrastructure Reliability Engineering** · **Team Scaling & Coaching**

1. (Reliability) "Walk me through the worst production incident you owned end-to-end. What changed in the system and in the team afterwards?" — Probes: What did you measure before/after? Who wrote the postmortem?
2. (Reliability) "Tell me about a time you had to argue for reliability work against feature pressure. How did you make the case, and what happened?" — Probes: What did you trade away? What would you do differently?
3. (Coaching) "Describe a platform engineer you grew significantly. What was your specific contribution to their growth?" — Probe: What did they struggle with 6 months in?
4. (Coaching) "Tell me about a time on-call was hurting your team. What did you concretely change?" — Probes: Rotation design? Alert hygiene numbers?
5. Candidate questions + share how our platform team works today (15 min).

Scoring: evidence-based notes per question; no gut scores; flag any answer with no concrete situation, metric, or personal contribution.
