# Job Description Agent — Question Bank

> STATUS: 🟡 v1 (2026-07-11, from Dana) — working set for tests; will be enhanced. Consumed by the JD agent per `job-description-PRD.md` §3: agent asks ONLY items the HM's open brief didn't answer, one at a time.

**Opening prompt (always):**
> Please describe the role and its purpose in as much detail as possible, in your own words. *(typed or as a recording)*

Tags: `[public]` = may appear in the JD directly · `[internal]` = informs the JD but never appears verbatim (PRD §6).

## A. Role basics
| # | Question | Tag |
|---|---|---|
| A1 | What is the title of the role? | public |
| A2 | Where is this role located? (Onsite, hybrid, remote — and any geographic requirements?) | public |
| A3 | Is this an Individual Contributor or a Manager role? If a Manager, how many direct reports and at what levels? | public |

## B. Company context
| # | Question | Tag |
|---|---|---|
| B1 | Can you please provide details about the company, its mission, vision, etc.? | public |
| B2 | How would you describe your company's mission, values, and culture? | public |
| B3 | Do you have a taxonomy of skills or culture/values framework that you use internally? | internal |

## C. Origin & purpose  *(branch: new role vs replacement)*
| # | Question | Tag |
|---|---|---|
| C1 | Is this a new position or a replacement? | internal |
| C2 | *(if replacement)* What worked well and what didn't with the previous setup or person? | internal |
| C3 | *(if new)* What prompted the need now? (e.g., business growth, new initiative, restructuring) — please elaborate. | public |
| C4 | How does this role contribute to the team's objectives and the company's broader strategic priorities? | public |
| C5 | Can you share examples of how similar roles have driven impact in the past? | public |

## D. Scope & responsibilities
| # | Question | Tag |
|---|---|---|
| D1 | What major goals or projects will this role help accomplish in the next 6–12 months? | public |
| D2 | What are the top 3–5 responsibilities this person will own? | public |
| D3 | What are the primary day-to-day activities and decisions they are expected to handle? | public |
| D4 | Are there specific processes, workflows, or projects they will be accountable for? | public |
| D5 | What authority or resources does this role have to execute its responsibilities? | public |

## E. Org context  *(candor encouraged — agent states this stays internal)*
| # | Question | Tag |
|---|---|---|
| E1 | Who does this role report to? Are there any dotted-line relationships? | public |
| E2 | Please describe the manager's leadership style (hands-off, coaching, collaborative, directive). Be candid — the manager relationship is one of the most critical success factors. | internal |
| E3 | What is the size, structure, and culture of the team? Please be transparent — team collaboration is another key success factor. | internal (size/structure public) |
| E4 | How does this team interact with other departments or functions? Who are the key internal and external stakeholders? | public |

## F. Market & complexity
| # | Question | Tag |
|---|---|---|
| F1 | What markets, customer segments, or industries does this role engage with? | public |
| F2 | Are there specific regulatory, client, or industry complexities to be aware of? | public |

## G. Level & requirements
| # | Question | Tag |
|---|---|---|
| G1 | What is the expected seniority level for this role? (Junior, Mid, Senior, Executive) | public |
| G2 | If you have an internal leveling framework, where does this role sit? | internal |

| G5 | Are there any "nice-to-have" skills or experiences that would make someone stand out? | public |

## I. Success definition
| # | Question | Tag |
|---|---|---|

| I2 | How will their performance be measured or evaluated? | public |
