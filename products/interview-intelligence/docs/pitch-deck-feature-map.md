## Competitive must-haves the deck claims as unique (protect these in our design)
- Context-based JD & skills definition (no competitor has it — BrightHire "no context")
- Red flags + **interview continuity** (cross-interview memory)
- Skill-by-skill analysis with **evidence** attached to each score
- Final rationale + ask-LLM over the evidence base

## Table-stakes (competitors have them; we need parity)
Recording & transcription, interview plans/questions, real-time guidance, notes + scorecard pushed to ATS, interviewer coaching.

## Recurring buyer asks from the deck's validation interviews
Job-specific competency definitions (Netflix, Miro, Metaview-partner org), evidence collection (Miro), interviewer guidance, ATS integration (Greenhouse/Lever/Workday named), GDPR compliance + granular sensitive-data control.
